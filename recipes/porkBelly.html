Pork Belly

Description

Pork belly is a boneless cut of fatty meat from the belly of a pig. It is known for its rich flavor and tender texture, making it a popular choice in various cuisines around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of pork belly (in large pieces)
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves;
  • 1 large chopped onion;
  • 1 tablespoon of salt;
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper;
  • Juice of 1 lemon;
  • 2 bay leaves;
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika (sweet or spicy, your choice);
  • 1 cup of water;
  • Oil or olive oil for browning.
