Pasta with garlic and oil

Description

That simple and practical recipe that everyone loves: pasta with garlic and oil! The presence of golden garlic gives a unique flavor to this dish!

Ingredients

  • 5 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 5 tablespoons of oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mash the garlic well together with the salt, forming a paste.
  2. In a frying pan, place the crushed garlic and oil.
  3. Fry over medium heat without letting the garlic burn, just brown.
  4. Add the butter and let it boil a little, just to incorporate the flavor well, stirring constantly.
  5. Place over the pasta immediately.
